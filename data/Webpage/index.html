Interactive feeding that adapts to your pet’s routine and bowl status.

This webpage presents a smart pet feeding system that combines automation, sensing, and remote control across a feeder unit and a monitoring unit. The design emphasizes scheduled feeding, remote feeding, and smart monitoring with overfeeding prevention built in.

    How it works

    Two units, one coordinated feeding workflow.

    01

    Monitor

    The monitoring unit checks whether the pet is nearby and whether food consumption is happening.

    02

    Verify

    The feeder unit checks bowl weight before dispensing to prevent overfeeding.

    03

    Dispense

    The servo motor releases a controlled portion when the system confirms it is safe.

    04

    Notify

    If the pet has not eaten, the system can generate a visible alert for the owner.
